MUSIC PRODUCT LIMITED

Music Product is a multidisciplinary, independent media company focused on audio/visual art.
We are a record label, music distributor and publisher based in Eastbourne, East Sussex.

Formed in 2019, Music Product started as a record label, but now has many
artists including producers, songwriters, vocal performers, digital artists,
fashion designers and photographers.
