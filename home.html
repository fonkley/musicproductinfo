MUSIC PRODUCT LIMITED

Music Product is a multidisciplinary, independent media company focused on audio/visual art. We are a record label, media distributor and collective based in Eastbourne, East Sussex. Formed in 2019, Music Product started as a record label created by Fonkley Honeysett, but now has many artists including producers, songwriters, vocal performers, digital artists, fashion designers and photographers.
